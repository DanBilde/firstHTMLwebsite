Some traditional romanian food

Sarmale

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chopped pork meat
  • 2 onions
  • parsley
  • 200 grams rice
  • 2 spoons tomato sauce
  • 1 cabbage
  • salt,pepper,chili

Preparation method

  1. Mix the chopped meat with the rice , chopped onion , parsley , tomato sauce , salt and pepper.
  2. Take 1 cabbage leaf at a time and fill it with the mixture above.

Mamaliga cu branza

Ingredients

  • 350 g corn flour
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 250 g cheese
  • 2 spoons of cream
  • salt

Preparation method

  1. Heat the oven at 150 degrees Celsius.
  2. Mix the corn flour with the butter , some water and a bit of salt.
  3. Add 1 layer of composition and then sprinkle some cheese on top of it.
  4. Add another layer and sprinkle some cheese again.
  5. Put in into the oven for 8-12 mins.
